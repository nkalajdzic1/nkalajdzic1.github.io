Mobile app development

I created this app while I was on the course "Mobile App Development" on the Faculty of Electrical Engineering in Sarajevo. The app was created in kotlin and is a quiz app for students. Firstly, upon start, the app fetches data from the faculty API for the given student and loades quizes: all quizes, past quizes, quizes for the given student and etc.

After the app has loaded all the necessary data, the student has two options: to filter and view the list of quizes or to sing up to a new group so the given student has more quizes to take. The student can only open quizes and answer and view the questions on which he is signed in.

All the answers get saved for the given quiz that the student is taking in a local database and when the student finishes the quiz, the answers are sent to the API and saved on the Back End. The student can upon answering view his result by opening the quiz and see what he/she/they answered right or just simply by seeing the final score in the list of quizes (in the list each quiz will contain the number of points if the quiz is finished.). The student can also answer only some of the questions, not all, and then continue later on.
